04 — questions
Common questions
Do I need to know how to code?

No. Every template comes with a setup wizard that asks you plain-language questions and generates your steps for you. If you can fill in a form, you can set it up.

What is n8n (and Make, Zapier)?

They're tools that connect your apps and run tasks automatically — like "when a form is submitted, add a row to my sheet." You don't build them from scratch; our templates drop straight in, and most have free plans.

What does "runs free" mean?

It means the template fits inside the free tier of the tool it runs on, so you can use it without paying monthly fees. Where a template could cost money at high volume, we tell you exactly when and how much before you buy.

What if it doesn't work for me?

Every template is tested before it's listed, and setup help is included. If you hit a wall, reach out and we'll help you get it running.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. If a template doesn't work as described and we can't fix it with you, we'll refund your purchase. Full refund terms will be linked at checkout.

What exactly do I download?

A ready-to-run pack: the template file you import into your tool, a plain-language setup guide, and the wizard. No subscriptions to us — you own what you download.
