An instant bridge from any app to Slack

Point any tool that can send a webhook — a form, a payment, a monitoring alert, a new signup — at this template, and it lands as a clean, formatted message in the Slack channel you choose.

The included wizard asks you plain-language questions (which channel, what the message should say, which fields to show) and hands you a ready-to-import workflow. You never touch code, and there's no server to maintain.
