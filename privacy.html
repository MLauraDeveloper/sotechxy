Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sotechxy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit www.sotechxy.com or buy from us. We keep the data we collect to a minimum and never sell it.

The short version: We collect your name and email so we can deliver what you buy and send updates you ask for. We don't sell your data, we don't track you with cookies, and payment details go straight to our secure payment provider — we never see or store your card number.

What we collect

We only collect information you give us directly:

  • Name — when you make a purchase.
  • Email address — when you sign up for updates or make a purchase.
  • Order information — a record of which template or pack you bought.

We do not collect sensitive information (such as government IDs, health data, or biometric data), we do not use cookies or tracking technologies, and we do not build profiles or make automated decisions about you.

How we use it

We use your information only to:

  • Deliver the templates and services you purchase.
  • Fulfill and manage your orders.
  • Respond to your questions and provide support.
  • Send administrative messages (like receipts and download links).
  • Send you marketing and promotional emails if you signed up — you can unsubscribe anytime.
  • Understand general usage trends and protect our service against fraud.

Legal bases (for EU/UK visitors)

If you're in the EU or UK, we process your information under these lawful bases: your consent (for marketing emails), performance of a contract (to deliver what you buy), our legitimate interests (to improve and protect our service), and legal obligations where the law requires it. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Who we share it with

We never sell your personal information. We share it only with the trusted service providers we need to run the business, and only so they can do their job:

  • Payment processors — to handle purchases securely. They process your payment on their own secure systems; we never see or store your card details.
  • Email/marketing tools — to send the updates you signed up for.
  • Website hosting providers — to serve this website to you.

We may also share information if required by law, or in connection with a business transfer (such as a merger or sale of the business).

International transfers

We operate from Chile, and some of our service providers are based in the United States. This means your information may be processed outside your own country, including outside the EU or UK. Where required, our providers rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses to protect information transferred internationally.

How long we keep it

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here — generally up to one year after your last interaction with us — unless a longer period is required by law (for example, for tax or accounting records). When we no longer need it, we delete or anonymize it.

How we protect it

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including serving this site securely over HTTPS, relying on reputable providers for payments and data storage, and limiting the data we collect in the first place. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to keep your information safe.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including the EU, UK, Canada, and US states such as California), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct information that is inaccurate.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below and we'll respond in line with applicable law.

Children

Our services are intended for adults and are not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date at the top. Significant changes will be communicated where appropriate.

Contact us

Email: hello@sotechxy.com

This policy is provided in good faith and in plain language. Sotechxy is a small independent business; as it grows and formally registers, this policy will be reviewed and updated accordingly.
